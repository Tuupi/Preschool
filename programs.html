Programs

Programs

There are three preschool programs.

Daycare

Daycare program is designed for providing stimulation, interaction, routine and exploration opportunities for early motor, social, intellectual and moral development.

Pre-Kindergarten

Pre-Kindergarten program is designed to provide opportunities for children to develop holistically.

Kindergarten

Kindergarten program is designed to foster academic growth, social development, and prepares children for a successful transition to elementary school.

Our Facilities

These are the preschool facilities.

Library

Our preschool boasts a vibrant and inviting library, filled with a diverse collection of books, fostering a love for reading and encouraging young minds to explore the world of literature.

Outdoor Playground

Our preschool features an expansive and engaging outdoor playground, providing a safe and stimulating environment for children to develop gross motor skills, socialize, and enjoy active play.

Cafetaria

Our preschool offers a welcoming and child-friendly cafeteria, where nutritious meals and snacks are served, promoting healthy eating habits and creating a positive dining experience for our young learners.
